Partick Window

View Our Featured Items
  • Stained Glass
  • Partick Window
  • Front mosaic panels

Each piece in our collection is a beautiful expression of someone's cherished memory of Partick. Etched with scenes that hold special significance to individuals, our artwork captures the essence of the Uni Cafe, The City Bakery, the mill at Partick Cross, The Steamie, and even the beloved Sacha the dog! These memories create a captivating tapestry of the neighborhood's history, culture, and the stories that have shaped it over the years. Each etching is a labor of love, preserving the nostalgia and heritage of Partick for generations to come. We are delighted to showcase these wonderful expressions of people's memories and celebrate the unique charm that makes Partick so special. Join us on this artistic journey and immerse yourself in the heartwarming narratives that these etchings unveil!
