Join the Friends of VICTORIA Park GLASGOW for an exciting sketching event in the beautiful VICTORIA Park. Led by the skilled professional artist, Rowena Comrie, the event will take place on July 23rd and August 6th at 2 pm. The July 23rd session is designed for experienced artists familiar with pencils, oil pastels, or other sketching materials, while the August 6th event welcomes budding artists. To attend, simply email fovpglasgow@gmail.com with 'Sketch' in the subject line. Spaces are limited and will be allocated on a first-come basis. Children are welcome but must be accompanied by an adult. Don't miss this unique opportunity to unleash your creativity amidst the park's scenic wonders!

Join YOUNITY for the Wee Partick Picnic on Saturday, July 22nd, 2023, at Mansfield Park from 1 pm to 4 pm. The event promises a delightful time with local musicians, a scrumptious Wee Picnic Lunch, flowers, and bubbles. Participants can enjoy various activities, including a Skipping Challenge, Circus skills, Kids Creative Play, Lavender Pot Planting, Face Painting, and a Chill Out Zone. Craft Tables will be available for those feeling crafty, and the Happy Camera Club Photo Exhibition will showcase captivating photographs. The event is organized in collaboration with Glasgow Annexe Communities, Partick Healthy Living Centre, City Council, Operation Partick Community Growing Project, and Play Outdoors. Come and celebrate a day of community bonding, fun, and creativity at the Wee Partick Picnic!

Join the Partick Community Growing Project for a serene and enjoyable Summer CHILL event on Sunday, July 9th, 2023, from 1 pm to 4 pm at THE PLOT. The event offers a range of calming activities, including Complementary Therapies like Reiki, Facials, and Indian Head Massage. For those interested in crafting, there will be a Silver Ring Making Workshop (booking required via email). To book a place for the workshop, kindly email meandersmeanders@gmail.com. Participants can also take part in Calming Summer Plug Planting of Lavender, Lemon Balm, and Rosemary, and enjoy community food and smoothies. The event is made possible by the Partick Thornwood Ideas Fund, and it's the perfect opportunity to relax and take time out in the company of friends and neighbors. Come and unwind with YOUNITY at the Summer CHILL event!
